Anime to Watch

Watchlist Trending

Demon Slayer

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is killed by demons. He strives to protect his sister Nezuko, who has turned into a demon.

My Hero Academia

My Hero Academia takes place in a world where nearly everyone has superpowers, or "quirks". It follows Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without a quirk, who dreams of becoming a great hero.

One Piece

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they search for the ultimate treasure, the "One Piece", in order to become the Pirate King.

Oshi no Ko

Oshi no Ko centers around the world of idols, fame, and revenge. It follows the story of a doctor who is reincarnated as the child of a famous idol.

Assassination Classroom

Assassination Classroom is about a group of students tasked with assassinating their strange and powerful teacher, Koro-sensei, before he destroys the Earth.

Bungo Stray Dogs

Bungo Stray Dogs follows a group of detectives who have supernatural abilities, solving cases while facing off against powerful criminal organizations.

Hell's Paradise

Hell's Paradise tells the story of a disgraced shinobi, Gabimaru, who embarks on a dangerous journey to a mysterious island to find the secret to immortality.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure follows the Joestar family through generations, each member with unique supernatural abilities, battling enemies in eccentric and flamboyant adventures.

Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul revolves around Ken Kaneki, a college student who is transformed into a half-ghoul after a near-fatal encounter with a ghoul, and struggles to find his identity in a world where humans and ghouls are in conflict.
