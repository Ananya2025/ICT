Anime Character Gallery

Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto is the main protagonist of the anime *Naruto*. He is an energetic and determined ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage to gain the recognition of his village.

Sailor Moon

Sailor Moon, also known as Usagi Tsukino, is a magical girl who transforms into Sailor Moon to protect Earth from evil forces. She is brave, loving, and often clumsy.

Monkey D. Luffy

Luffy is the main character in *One Piece*. He has the power of the Gum-Gum Fruit and his dream is to become the Pirate King by finding the legendary One Piece treasure.

Goku

Goku is the protagonist of *Dragon Ball Z*. A Saiyan warrior with incredible strength, Goku is always seeking to improve himself through battles and training.
