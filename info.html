Demon Slayer

Genres: Action, Dark Fantasy, Supernatural Studio: Ufotable Aired: 2019 – present (ongoing) Plot: Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who lives with his family in the mountains. When he returns from selling charcoal one day, he finds his family slaughtered by demons. To make matters worse, his sister Nezuko has been turned into a demon. Tanjiro becomes a demon slayer to protect Nezuko and seek revenge against the demon who killed his family. Along the way, he encounters other demon slayers and learns the brutal truths about demons.

My Hero Academia

Genres: Action, Superhero, School, Shounen Studio: Bones Aired: 2016 – present (ongoing) Plot: In a world where nearly every human has a "quirk" (a special ability), Izuku Midoriya is born without one. However, he dreams of becoming a great hero like his idol, All Might. After a chance encounter with All Might, Midoriya is chosen to inherit his mentor’s quirk and is enrolled at U.A. High School, where he trains to become a professional hero. Throughout his journey, Midoriya and his classmates face increasingly dangerous threats from villains and discover the true nature of heroism.

One Piece

Genres: Adventure, Action, Fantasy Studio: Toei Animation Aired: 1999 – present (ongoing) Plot: One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a boy whose body gained the properties of rubber after eating the Gum-Gum Fruit, a type of "Devil Fruit." He embarks on a grand adventure with his diverse pirate crew, the Straw Hat Pirates, in search of the legendary treasure known as the One Piece. His goal is to become the Pirate King, but along the way, he must face powerful foes, unravel the mysteries of the world, and protect his friends.

Oshi no Ko

Genres: Drama, Mystery, Psychological, Supernatural Studio: Doga Kobo Aired: 2023 Plot: Oshi no Ko is set in the entertainment industry, focusing on the world of idols, fame, and reincarnation. The story begins when a doctor is reincarnated as one of the children of a famous idol, Ai Hoshino. The doctor’s memories from his previous life remain intact, and he, along with his twin sister, investigates the dark side of the idol industry while navigating their complex new lives as "children of the star." The series explores themes of fame, obsession, and the harsh reality of the entertainment world.

Assassination Classroom

Genres: Action, Comedy, School, Supernatural Studio: Lerche Aired: 2015 – 2016 Plot: The story of Assassination Classroom revolves around a group of middle school students who are tasked with assassinating their strange teacher, Koro-sensei, who has destroyed 70% of the moon and threatens to do the same to Earth. Koro-sensei is an alien-like being with superhuman strength, speed, and the ability to regenerate, making him nearly impossible to kill. Despite his threatening demeanor, he is an excellent teacher who helps his students grow, both academically and emotionally.

Bungo Stray Dogs

Genres: Action, Mystery, Supernatural, Seinen Studio: Bones Aired: 2016 – present (ongoing) Plot: Bungo Stray Dogs follows the story of Atsushi Nakajima, a young man who is kicked out of his orphanage and becomes involved with a group of individuals with supernatural abilities. This group, known as the Armed Detective Agency, uses their powers to solve mysterious cases while battling against powerful criminal organizations. Many of the characters are named after famous authors and have abilities related to their works.

Hell's Paradise

Genres: Action, Adventure, Supernatural, Dark Fantasy Studio: Mappa Aired: 2023 Plot: Hell's Paradise follows Gabimaru, a skilled shinobi who is sentenced to death but is sent on a dangerous mission to a mysterious island to find the secret to immortality. Along with other condemned criminals, Gabimaru encounters terrifying monsters and otherworldly dangers. As he searches for the elixir of life, he must confront his own morality and desire for redemption.

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure

Genres: Action, Supernatural, Adventure, Shounen Studio: David Production Aired: 2012 – present (ongoing) Plot: JoJo's Bizarre Adventure spans multiple generations of the Joestar family, each with their own unique supernatural abilities. The family members battle powerful enemies across different timelines and locations, using their "Stands" (manifestations of their fighting spirits). The series is known for its over-the-top battles, vibrant art style, and eccentric characters.

Tokyo Ghoul

Genres: Horror, Psychological, Supernatural, Action Studio: Pierrot Aired: 2014 – 2018 Plot: Tokyo Ghoul follows Ken Kaneki, a college student who survives a near-fatal encounter with a ghoul, a flesh-eating monster. He is transformed into a half-ghoul and must navigate a world where humans and ghouls are in conflict. Kaneki struggles with his new identity while being drawn into the dangerous and violent world of ghouls.
